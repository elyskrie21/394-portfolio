MoonDeal

What is MoonDeal

Moondeal is a chrome extenstion/webiste that notifies users when particular products go on sale. Users are able to select products from popular online shops such as Amazon, Target, Ebay, etc. We created this extension because many online shopers have to constantly check if their products go on sale and may have missed out on deals.
